RCVJ, Incorporated was established on October 09, 1992, founded by Hon. Recto M. Cantimbuhan as President and his wife Mrs. Veronica J. Cantimbuhan as Treasurer and Vice President.

For over three decades, RCVJ has been providing exceptional manpower and janitorial services to prestigious companies across Cavite, Laguna, and other key locations in Luzon.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and ethical business practices has established us as a trusted partner in the industry, connecting businesses with top talent and providing comprehensive staffing solutions.
