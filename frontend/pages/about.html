RCVJ, Incorporated was established on October 09, 1992, with Hon. Recto M. Cantimbuhan as President and his wife Mrs. Veronica J. Cantimbuhan as Treasurer and Vice President.

For over three decades, RCVJ has been providing exceptional manpower and janitorial services to prestigious companies across Cavite, Laguna, and other key locations in Luzon. Our commitment to quality and reliability has made us a trusted partner in the industry.

Our Mission

RCVJ Manpower Agency connects businesses with the right people and talents they need. We provide reliable staffing solutions, skilled workers, and professional support to help you build a stronger workforce and achieve success with confidence.

Our Services

Manpower Services

Providing skilled and reliable workforce solutions for various industries across Luzon.

Janitorial Services

Staffing Solutions

Connecting businesses with qualified professionals for temporary and permanent positions.
